# Lab use

Day-to-day expectations for Connection Lab members at Boelter Hall 3440.

## Members agreement (2026–2027)

Full document: **[Connection Lab Members Agreement 2026–2027](/connection-lab-members-agreement-2026-2027.pdf)** (PDF)

Brief outline:

1. **Purpose** — Expectations for all Connection Lab members (including IRI and affiliated researchers). Signing confirms commitment to a collaborative, respectful research environment.

2. **Quiet hours (weekdays, 9 AM–6 PM)**
   - Keep conversation volume low; use whiteboards for active collaboration.
   - Take phone calls and long conversations to meeting areas outside the main lab.
   - Use the supply-closet desk/whiteboard for interviews, meetings, or focused quiet work.
   - Avoid disruptive noise; **one guest per member** (you are responsible for your guest).

3. **Outside quiet hours** — Guidelines are relaxed to encourage collaboration; use the space respectfully and in line with the lab’s mission.

4. **Facility & equipment**
   - Use equipment for your own research or academic work; handle it carefully.
   - Printing is mainly for lab-related academic/research materials; limit personal printing.
   - Dispose of food waste in the bin **outside** the lab entrance; keep lab bins clear.
   - Clean table surfaces after meals; if you use the sink, leave it clean.

5. **Events & student clubs**
   - Clubs may reserve the lab **Thursday–Sunday after 6 PM** only.
   - Clean up fully after use and restore furniture/equipment.
   - Misuse or repeated violations can revoke club access.

6. **Acknowledgment** — By entering with your Bruin Card or keypad code, you agree to follow this code of conduct.
